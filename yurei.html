Yurei

Yurei is a clothing brand focusing on t-shirts. I created the brand with the graphics first. The ghost was a creation from a challenge of drawing everyday.

Design

Here is the main t-shirt for the brand. The original one you could say. The design is based on a charismatic and sometimes optimistic ghost. Yurei means dim spirit or ghost in Japanese. I was always drawn to the Kawaii culture and thought why not create my own rendition.

The Brand

Clean and simple is important. The red symbolizes the red ink used on a hanko, a tradtional Kanji stamp. The logo is a simplified version of the Kanji characters for Yurei (as seen on my contact page).
